August 14(Mon.) - 19(Sat.), 2023

Incheon National University, Incheon, Republic of Korea

The aim of the conference is to contribute to the development of ring theory, strengthening ties of friendship and mutual understanding between ring theorists in China, Japan and Korea.

We also welcome experts from other countries to participate in the conference.

Main topics are Ring and Module, Representation Theory.
